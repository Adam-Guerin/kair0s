# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors

We expect all community participants to:

- **Be respectful and inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- **Use welcoming and inclusive language**: Avoid exclusionary language
- **Be empathetic**: Consider different perspectives and experiences
- **Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences**: Focus on what is best for the community
- **Gracefully accept constructive criticism**: Improve based on feedback
- **Focus on what is best for the community**: Put community interests first
- **Show empathy towards other community members**: Understand that everyone has different skill levels

### Unacceptable Behaviors

We do not tolerate:

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, sexual harassment, or bullying
- **Discriminatory language**: Use of sexualized language, or unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- **Trolling**: Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- **Private harassment**: Private harassment without public disclosure
- **Publishing private information**: Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- **Threats**: Any threats of violence or harm
- **Spam**: Unwanted promotional content or repetitive posting
- **Disruptive behavior**: Behavior that disrupts productive discussion

## Enforcement Responsibilities

### Community Leaders

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive,
or harmful.

### Scope

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are
not aligned to this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any
contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening,
offensive, or harmful.

## Enforcement

### Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns,
please report it to our community leaders:

- **Email**: conduct@kair0s.com
- **GitHub**: Report the issue and tag @kair0s/maintainers
- **Discord**: Message any moderator

### Response Process

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that
is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. Community leaders are
obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

### Consequences

Community leaders may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning,
temporary suspension, or permanent banning, depending on the severity and
nature of the behavior.

## 🤝 Our Commitment to Inclusion

At Kair0s, we believe that diverse perspectives and experiences strengthen our
project and community. We are committed to:

- **Welcoming contributors of all backgrounds**: Regardless of experience level, geographic location, or personal circumstances
- **Providing equal opportunities**: Everyone should have the same opportunity to contribute and succeed
- **Creating a safe environment**: Where people feel comfortable sharing ideas and asking questions
- **Supporting growth**: Helping community members learn and develop their skills
- **Recognizing contributions**: Acknowledging and celebrating all forms of contribution

## 📚 Getting Help

If you're unsure whether something is appropriate or need guidance:

- **Ask first**: When in doubt, ask for clarification
- **Start discussions**: Use GitHub Discussions for questions
- **Seek mentorship**: We're happy to help newcomers learn
- **Report concerns**: Don't hesitate to report issues

## 🎯 Specific Guidelines

### Code Reviews

- **Be constructive**: Focus on the code, not the person
- **Explain reasoning**: Help others understand your suggestions
- **Be patient**: Remember everyone has different experience levels
- **Acknowledge good work**: Recognize thoughtful contributions

### Issue Discussions

- **Stay on topic**: Keep discussions focused on the issue at hand
- **Be respectful**: Even when disagreeing, maintain respect
- **Provide context**: Help others understand your perspective
- **Seek understanding**: Ask clarifying questions when needed

### Community Spaces

- **Be welcoming**: Help newcomers feel included
- **Share knowledge**: Contribute to collective learning
- **Stay professional**: Maintain professional conduct in all spaces
- **Report issues**: Help maintain a healthy community environment
